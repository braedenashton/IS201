About Me

This is what I do.

I was born in Las Vegas Nevada on July 29, 2000. It wasn't until around 2006 that I was introduced to Bionicles and the mighty Toa. My home boy Adam introduced me to them and that changed my life.

After having played with bionicles for about 5 years, my family moved to Highland, Utah, and then 10 months later to Houston, Texas. Even while we moved around we brought our Bionicles with us. Living in so many places was a great expereince for me and helped me to learn how to make friends, be happy wherever I am, and appreciate diversity in people.

I finished high school in Highland and then served a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in the Washington Everett Mission. I could not bring my Bionicles there. I have been attending BYU since I returned home and still have my Bionicles. I am studying Experience Design and Management.
